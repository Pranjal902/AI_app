
# Linguistic Forensic Biometrics (2026)

VoxVeritas identifies "Mathematical Perfection" in speech, which is the primary indicator of synthetic origin.

## 🌏 Global Synthetic Markers
- **Mathematical Prosody**: AI voices often exhibit a metronomic regularity in syllable timing. Biological speech has chaotic micro-fluctuations (jitter).
- **Spectral Quantization**: AI synthesis models (especially older ones) leave "stepping" patterns in high-frequency bands (16-24kHz).
- **Inhalation Latency**: AI often adds "canned" breaths that occur at mathematically precise intervals.

## 🇮🇳 Language-Specific Phonetic Audit

### Tamil & Malayalam (Retroflex Focus)
- **Articulatory Depth**: The complex positioning for retroflex sounds (like 'ழ' or 'ള') creates a unique spectral fingerprint. AI often simplifies these, resulting in a "flat" phonetic resonance.

### Hindi & Telugu (Aspiration Focus)
- **Mahaprana Verification**: The burst of air in aspirated sounds like 'ख' or 'ध' is extremely difficult for synthesis to replicate with biological randomness. AI bursts are either too uniform or lack the requisite "breath-to-tone" ratio.

### English (Global Nuance)
- **Glottal Smoothing**: Synthetic English often lacks the "vocal fry" or non-standard glottal stops found in natural conversation, sounding "too clean" for forensic standards.
